=== ABOUT THE STORE ===

Welcome to Nour Store — a Moroccan online fashion boutique based in Casablanca.
We sell women's and men's casual and semi-formal clothing, including djellabas, abayas, traditional kaftans, modern streetwear, and accessories.
All our products are curated from local Moroccan manufacturers and selected international suppliers.
Our website: www.nour-store.ma
We ship across all of Morocco and process orders 7 days a week.

=== SHIPPING POLICY ===

We deliver across all of Morocco. Standard delivery takes 2–5 business days depending on your city.

Cities with 2–3 day delivery: Casablanca, Rabat, Salé, Témara, Kenitra, Marrakech, Fès, Meknès, Tanger, Agadir, Oujda, Tétouan.

Remote areas (Laâyoune, Dakhla, Errachidia, Zagora, Guelmim) may take 5–7 business days.

Delivery to rural addresses (douar, commune rurale) may take up to 10 days.

Shipping is FREE on orders above 400 MAD.

Orders below 400 MAD have a flat shipping fee of 35 MAD.

You will receive an SMS with a tracking link once your order is dispatched.

If you haven't received your SMS within 24 hours of placing your order, please contact support.

We currently ship within Morocco only. International shipping is not available.


=== CASH ON DELIVERY (COD) ===

Cash on delivery is available nationwide. You pay the delivery agent in cash when you receive your order.

COD orders require a phone number that is reachable — the delivery agent will call before arriving.

If the delivery agent cannot reach you after 2 attempts, the order is returned and a reshipping fee of 35 MAD applies.

Please make sure the exact amount is ready — delivery agents may not carry change.

COD is not available for orders above 2000 MAD. For large orders, card payment is required.


=== RETURNS & REFUNDS ===

You can return any item within 14 days of the delivery date.

Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging with tags attached.

Worn, washed, or damaged items will not be accepted for return.

To initiate a return:
1. Contact us on WhatsApp at +212-6XX-XXXXXX with your order number and reason for return.
2. We will arrange a pickup — you do not need to go to a post office.
3. Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund is processed within 5–7 business days.

Refund methods:
- Card payments → refunded to original card.
- COD orders → refunded via bank transfer or store credit (your choice).

Items that CANNOT be returned:
- Underwear, swimwear, and intimate apparel (hygiene reasons).
- Custom or personalized items unless defective.
- Items purchased during final sale promotions (marked "Non-retournable").

If you received a wrong or defective item, we will replace it at no cost. Send us a photo on WhatsApp.


=== EXCHANGES ===

We offer exchanges within 14 days of delivery for a different size or color, subject to availability.

To request an exchange, contact us on WhatsApp with your order number and the item you want instead.

If the replacement item costs more, you pay the difference. If it costs less, we issue store credit.

Exchange shipping is free if the original item was delivered correctly.


=== PAYMENT METHODS ===

We accept:
- Cash on delivery (COD) — available nationwide.
- Credit and debit cards: Visa, Mastercard (Moroccan and international cards).
- CMI online payment (Moroccan bank cards).
- Store credit issued from previous refunds or exchanges.

We do NOT accept:

- Cryptocurrency.
- Bank transfer for new orders (only for refunds).

All prices are listed in Moroccan Dirhams (MAD). No hidden fees.


=== ORDER TRACKING ===

You can track your order using the SMS link sent to your phone after dispatch.

If the link doesn't work or you didn't receive an SMS, contact us with your order number and we'll check manually.

Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement if not yet shipped. After that, cancellation is not possible.

If your order has been "Processing" for more than 48 hours, contact support — this is not normal.


=== DELIVERY ISSUES ===

If your package arrives damaged, take photos immediately and contact us within 24 hours.

If the tracking shows "Delivered" but you haven't received anything, check with neighbors first, then contact us within 48 hours.

If the delivery failed because you were unavailable, the carrier will attempt redelivery once. After that, you must collect from their local agency or request reshipping (35 MAD fee).

=== FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ===

Q: Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?
A: Yes, but only within 1 hour of placing the order and if it hasn't been dispatched yet. Contact us immediately on WhatsApp.

Q: I ordered the wrong size. What do I do?
A: Contact us within 1 hour of placing the order to correct it for free. If the order is already dispatched, you'll need to go through the exchange process after delivery.

Q: Can I order by phone or WhatsApp instead of the website?
A: Yes. Send us a message on WhatsApp with the product name, size, color, and your delivery address. We'll confirm availability and process your order manually.

Q: How do I know my size?
A: Refer to our size guide above, or send us your measurements on WhatsApp and we'll advise.

Q: The item I received doesn't match the photo. What do I do?
A: Send us a photo on WhatsApp within 24 hours. If there's a genuine color or design mismatch beyond normal screen variation, we'll arrange a free replacement or full refund.

Q: Can I buy as a gift? Do you offer gift wrapping?
A: Yes, we offer gift wrapping for 20 MAD extra. Add a note at checkout with the recipient's name and we'll include a handwritten card.

Q: I haven't received my order and it's been 6 days.
A: First check your SMS tracking link. If the status shows no movement for 48 hours, contact us — we'll open an investigation with the carrier.

Q: Are your djellabas true to size?
A: Our djellabas are sized by height, not weight. Check the size guide above. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a traditional drape, or staying at your size for a more fitted look.
